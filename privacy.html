🌲 Larson Family Finance — Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026 · Operated by the Larson household (developer of record: Brigham Larson Pianos, Orem, Utah)

Larson Family Finance is a private, household-only financial application. It is not offered to the public; its only users are members of one family. This policy describes how the application handles their data.

What we collect

  • Financial statement data that household members enter directly (asset and liability line items, income figures, budgeting envelopes, family ledger entries).
  • Bank account and transaction data retrieved through Plaid, only for accounts a household member chooses to connect, and only after that member authenticates directly with their bank and consents inside Plaid Link.
  • We do not collect or store Social Security numbers, dates of birth, or bank login credentials. Bank credentials are entered only in Plaid's own interface and are never visible to this application.

How data is used

Solely for the household's own budgeting, spending reports, and preparation of the family's personal financial statement. Data is never sold, rented, shared with advertisers, or disclosed to any third party other than the service providers that operate the application (Netlify for hosting and storage; Plaid for bank connectivity).

How data is protected

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit with TLS 1.2 or better (HTTPS).
  • Data is stored in Netlify Blobs, which encrypts data at rest and in transit.
  • Access requires authentication; only authorized household members can sign in.
  • Plaid access tokens and application secrets are held in server-side environment variables, never in client code or the code repository.

Consent

Each bank connection is initiated by the account owner personally, who authenticates with their bank and grants consent through Plaid Link at the moment of connection. Connections can be revoked at any time at my.plaid.com or by contacting us.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained while the household actively uses the application. Any household member may request correction or deletion of their data at any time by emailing the contact below; deletion requests are honored within 30 days, including removal of stored transactions and revocation of the related Plaid access tokens. This policy is reviewed at least annually.
